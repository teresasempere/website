A complex signage system for a particular University dental clinic

During the graphic design practices that I did in the architecture studio 'Adam Bresnick Architects,' I had the opportunity to design and develop a complete signage system for the CEU San Pablo University Dental Clinic.

The designed system had to take into account the peculiarities of the space, almost a labyrinthine, the particular materials, glass, PVC, and the aesthetics of the architectural project.

In turn, taking into account the accessibility of the signals was essential, their clarity and easy understanding were vital.

In the next images, I show the sketches and diagrams made during the development of the project and some photos of the final result. For more information, you can visit the online portfolio of 'Adam Bresnick Architects'.
